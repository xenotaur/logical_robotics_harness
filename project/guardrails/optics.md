# Optics Guardrails

## Initial rules

- Avoid actions that can misrepresent evidence or status.
- Escalate actions that may affect external trust, compliance posture, or stakeholder confidence.
- Ensure status narratives clearly distinguish facts, assumptions, and pending verification.
